About us

Launched in 2021, it is a platform tailored for the owners of small to medium scale businesses providing them with easy, accessible online point of sale system.
We believe handling an up and coming business should be easy, accessible, and enjoyable.

At Bukas, we aim to provide every small-medium business in the Philippines a way to innovate their way of business.

Filipino entrepreneurs need to know that managing a business no longer needs to be done manually.  Bukas would help them start in the right path towards the better future of their business by providing them the necessary tools to strengthen the foundations of their developing businesses.
